Teacher's Profile
Credentials & background

Teacher Nadi Yoon Htet (Khloe), Founder of NEXIS and IELTS Instructor, is a passionate English language educator dedicated to helping students achieve their IELTS goals. Having taken the IELTS test three times and earning overall band scores of 8.0, 8.5, and 9.0, Khloe brings a wealth of personal experience and professional training to her teaching. She is committed to providing high-quality, student-centered instruction that empowers learners to succeed.

Mission

At NEXIS, we are dedicated to:

  • Delivering Excellence in IELTS Training: Providing structured, effective, and personalized lessons that equip students with the skills and confidence to achieve their target bands.
  • Fostering Independent Learners: Encouraging students to develop critical thinking, self-discipline, and lifelong learning habits that extend beyond the IELTS exam.
  • Building a Supportive Learning Community: Creating an environment where learners feel motivated, guided, and inspired to reach their full potential.
  • Integrating Real Experience with Modern Teaching: Combining real IELTS insights, data-driven strategies, and student-centered teaching methods to ensure measurable progress.
  • Promoting Global Communication: Helping students master English as a bridge to academic, professional, and personal opportunities worldwide.

Vision

To empower candidates by

  • Becoming a Leading Learning Platform: Establishing NEXIS as a trusted name in IELTS and English education recognized for excellence, innovation, and integrity.
  • Redefining IELTS Preparation: Shifting the focus from rote learning to meaningful, skill-based education that develops real-world communication and confidence.
  • Integrating Modern Teaching Approaches: Combining technology, research-based methods, and personalized instruction to enhance the learning experience.
  • Inspiring Lifelong Learners: Encouraging students to view English not just as a test requirement, but as a lifelong tool for global connection and personal advancement.
  • Driving Positive Change Through Education: Using the power of language learning to open doors, build opportunities, and inspire individuals to achieve their dreams.
